Online Second Opinion Program

A diagnosis of cancer can be life changing. Many patients desire, and may benefit from, a second opinion. However, travelling to another treatment center can be time consuming, costly, and challenging. Let VeritOnc provide peace of mind as you embark on your journey to cure, all from the convenience of your home. We provide a rapid, comprehensive, and confidential case assessment with evaluation of your cancer type, treatment options, answers to your questions, and information regarding available experimental treatments in your area.

At Veritonc, we believe that all patients deserve a second opinion.

Do you feel that you do not fully understand your treatment options? Do you feel that physician recommending treatment may be biased? Would you benefit from further education regarding the best treatments personalized for your specific disease and most importantly, your personal priorities?

We hope you consider us for your online second opinion and know we are always here ready to serve you and walk with you through your fight against cancer.

How it works

  1. Create an online VeritOnc account
  2. Choose the right program for you
    1. Phone Conversation (1 hour) $350 Discuss with a radiation oncologist about your treatment diagnosis, the disease, side effects, changes in life style and other questions you may have. Due to the schedule of our physicians, phone conversations are limited to 1 hour.
    2. Video Conference (1 Hour) $400 Join an online video conference with one of our radiation oncologists. Have an open ended conversation with them regarding your diagnosis, treatment record, therapy plan, options and questions you may have. Due to the schedule of our physicians video conferences are limited to 1 hour.
    3. Full Second Opinion $600 ($500 is patient provides medical records) One of our radiation oncologists will look at your specific case, medical records and recommended treatment plan from your primary physician and provide a second opinion of the diagnosis and treatment plan. You will receive a document stating the second opinion, information about the disease and any questions you may have had about the disease or treatment plan along the way.
  3. Submit the appropriate medical records relating to your cancer diagnosis for review. Have any questions or concerns about this process? Check out our Frequently Asked Questions for more detail, or contact us directly
  4. Receive your second opinion!

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I begin in getting my second opinion?

First you must create an account here (link) and choose a plan that best fits you. Once this is done you have the option of providing the medical records for a reduced fee or have us take care of collecting the records. 7-10 days after the records are received we will fulfill the service you requested.

How much does your service cost?

We have many plans with differing costs to provide you a service that is most beneficial to you and your schedule. These options range from $350 to $600. Please see our How it works page for further details. Unfortunately, we are unable to accept insurances for this service but can accept health savings account, Paypal or credit card payments.

How long will it take?

After we receive the medical records please provide us 7-10 business days for your service. There is a very realistic possibility you will receive it even sooner, however, due to the hectic schedule of our physicians we ask you afford us this timeline.

Can anyone use this service?

For the most part, yes. However, we cannot offer online second opinions to a small number of patients living in certain excluded states and countries, due to regulatory constraints. If you live in one of these areas, you can still request an in-person second opinion by filling out this online form or calling 502-123-4567.

Online Second Opinion Program

A diagnosis of cancer can be life changing. Many patients desire, and may benefit from, a second opinion. However, travelling to another treatment center can be time consuming, costly, and challenging. Let VeritOnc provide peace of mind as you embark on your journey to cure, all from the convenience of your home. We provide a rapid, comprehensive, and confidential case assessment with evaluation of your cancer type, treatment options, answers to your questions, and information regarding available experimental treatments in your area.

At Veritonc, we believe that all patients deserve a second opinion.

Do you feel that you do not fully understand your treatment options? Do you feel that the physician recommending treatment may be biased? Would you benefit from further education regarding the best treatments personalized for your specific disease and most importantly, your personal priorities?

We hope you consider us for your online second opinion and know we are always here ready to serve you and walk with you through your fight against cancer.

How it works

Create an online VeritOnc account.
Choose the right program for you.
Submit the appropriate medical records relating to your cancer diagnosis for review.
Receive your second opinion!
